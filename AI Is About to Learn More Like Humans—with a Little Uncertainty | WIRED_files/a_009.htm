            Nueral networks are easy to understand. In its most simplistic form a neural network is a landscape and your input is the starting destination and your desired output is the destination. This landscape starts off with random perturbations, but as you train your neural network you create paths through the landscape that slowly create a trail. The more you train it, the trail turns into a road, and the road into a freeway, until you get a path that no matter what input you get leads to a desired output. Just like a landscape, it takes a lot of data (walking through) to get a good path made.
