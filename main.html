CNC Mill

I design this CNC completely from scratch. I began by creating a model in sketchup and prototyping parts with a 3D printer. Once I had settled on the design, I printed the plastic parts and ordered all the hardware online. I used a 3D printer's controller board and software since I was already familiar with them. Once I had everything assembled I had to calibrate it and then test run it until I considered it to be in a functional state.
